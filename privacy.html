Privacy

This static site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to collect basic, aggregate information about visits to the site, such as page views, referrers, and general performance data. This is used to understand whether the site is being visited and to improve the product.

Free sample downloads are served through GitHub Pages. Payments and digital product delivery are handled by Payhip. Feedback is collected through Google Forms.

If you submit feedback, the information you provide may be used to improve the product. Do not include passwords, private coursework, API keys, or sensitive personal information in feedback forms.
